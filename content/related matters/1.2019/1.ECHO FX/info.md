title: Project Title
description: replicating brexit night through misinformation
---

ECHO FX replays the night of the Brexit vote through the echoes of disinformation spread on social media by nigel farage, the face of leave campaign; suggesting a remain win, those echoes manipulated market sentiment in order to inflate the value of the pound, seemingly setting it up for a pump-and-dump, a tactic to boost the value of an asset through false, misleading or exaggerated statements; as the pound subsequently collapsed under market disbelief, a number of london city traders with access to insider information pointing to a leave win made millions
