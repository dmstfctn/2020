---
title: The Network Ensemble
description: a noise sound project using network data
---

the NETWORK ENSEMBLE was a noise project making use of wireless network data as creative medium; the NETWORK ENSEMBLE consisted of four machines able to translate data captured from local wireless networks into sonic signals, and a piece of custom software able to further modulate these signals; performed by two human operators, the noise performances executed under the moniker explored the activity of local wireless networks through sound; the NETWORK ENSEMBLE is released by NUKFM and MILLE PLATEAUX
